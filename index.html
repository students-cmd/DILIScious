Ingredients

Bolinao (Anchovy) Fish, Rice Flour, Salt, Pepper, Vegetable Oil, Seasoning (may include spices such as garlic powder, onion powder).

Description

DILIScious is a savory, crispy snack made from freshly caught bolinaw (anchovy) fish, carefully seasoned and prepared to deliver a delicious, crunchy bite. Perfect for snacking anytime or as an addition to meals, this tasty treat is crafted with high-quality ingredients and a focus on flavor.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry place. Once opened, keep sealed to maintain freshness.
